Jefa

The E-Commerce Leather Bag Store


The Problem

In the digital world it is quite hard to compete with other well-known famous brands when you are entering the market to sell your products.

The Jefa team approached us to find a solution to promote, brand & sell their leather bag products across India and Abroad. Having marked upon their territory in the whole-sale business, they now wanted to communicate with their customers directly as well. Adroit Alliance had to find a solution to keep their whole-sale consumers updated about their products as well as sell in retail quantities directly at the end customer's door-step.

And of course, being a startup, there were budget constraints too!

Our Solution


Not bragging about it, but we really excel in making the best attractive, easy to use & super fast mobile applications and websites. For the wholesale consumers, we thought of making a perfect website which would display all the products category wise with individual product descriptions. The customers can simply view all of Jefa's leather bags and can communicate directly with the Jefa sales team through WhatsApp by just a click of a button on the website itself. Thus the customers can update themselves of the new bags available.
On the other hand, we created a beautiful looking Jefa Mobile Application from where customers can View, Like, Save & Order their favorite leather bags and get them delivered at their doorstep.
