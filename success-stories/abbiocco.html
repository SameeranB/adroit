Abbiocco

The Pre - Ordering Food App


The Problem


Most of our Indian population prefers going to the restaurant themselves to enjoy their food experience. This in turn promotes long queues at the restaurants for hours. The need of the hour was to satisfy every foodie out there wandering for food without wasting their time; either standing outside the restaurant or having to fill his hunger by looking at food being served on other tables while still waiting for his own food to be served.

Our Solution


Abbiocco is our first national product which aims at eradicating the time required to prepare food for the customers as well as helps in saving the customer's time by not keeping him wait for his own food at the restaurant.



  • Feature 1

    Search easily for Restaurants and Cafes in your location.

  • Feature 2

    View menus, user reviews, and all the other information you need to choose a restaurant for Dine In or Takeaway.

  • Feature 3

    Browse our theme-based curated lists to discover the best dishes of your favorite restaurants, offers provided by them and their happy hours.

  • Feature 4

    Rate and review restaurants you've been to.

  • Feature 5

    Maintain an easy reference list of your favorites or places you want to visit with favorite star marks.

  • Feature 6

    In-app pre-ordering of food at all partner restaurants which means your food will be ready by the time you reach.
