Paragi's Kitchen - PK

The Online Indian Food Guide

The Problem


Home cooked food in India has always been appreciated by everyone in the nation as well as abroad. Recently it was noticed that mother's wants to prepare delicious and healthy food for everyone in the house, but could not find the best recipes online out of the many recipes available from different chefs for the same food item. Turn the page to know what we came up with!

Our Solution


Paragi's Kitchen is a mobile application available on the playstore which provides the best recipes for every food item out of all the available recipes online. These recipes are first tried & tasted by Mrs. Paragi herself and then they are ranked according to their pros-n-cons. The app provides a plethora of cuisines under which there are various food items along with the video links of the best recipes available on YouTube.



  • Feature 1

    Get the best food recipes which are tried & tasted first.



  • Feature 2

    Choose cuisine type according to your mood.

  • Feature 3

    Get a variety of food items under the same cuisine.



  • Feature 4

    Forget the old school technique of reading recipes and switch to watching videos directly to make the food you want.
