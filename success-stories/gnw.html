Grab -n -Win

In-House Textile Monitoring System


The Problem


This Garment Company in Milan Mall, Mumbai, wanted to expand their business nationally and internationally and wanted to change their management system from the old pen-paper towards the new tech friendly approach. They contacted us and we were happy to help!

Our Solution


With the increasing demand of e-commerce websites for large and profitable sales, we designed and developed a website for GNW and marketed them such that their sales increased nationaly while they operated from mumbai itself.
The mobile app focuses on transferring all their written documents, bills, stocks, etc from the hardcopy to the app itself. Their staff can upload their new fabric purchases, bulk orders, bills, sent packages and other inhouse activities live on the app, thus track their progress and stay updated to the ongoings. The staff can update about their clothing fabrics which are sent /recieved for: Washing, Cutting, Making, Stocks, Finishing, Kaj Dept, Bills and Packaging



  • Feature 1

    Maintain your whole business on your finger tips.

  • Feature 2

    Upload & look after your new & old fabrics on the go.

  • Feature 3

    Keep a track of your fabrics that has gone for cutting, stitching, finishing & washing 24/7.

  • Feature 4

    Let all your invoices stay online so that you don't have to find them.

  • Feature 5

    Retain & update your stocks from anywhere, any time.

  • Feature 6

    Catch on your daily miscellaneous expenses such as tea/coffee/snacks, etc.
